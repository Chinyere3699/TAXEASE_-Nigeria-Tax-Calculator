# Taxease

## Overview
Taxease is a mobile-first tax management platform designed to help Nigerian employees, freelancers, SMEs, payroll officers, HR teams, accountants, and tax consultants calculate, understand, and manage their tax obligations using Nigerian Personal Income Tax rules.

The platform simplifies tax computation through transparent calculations, payroll support, tax education resources, and beginner-friendly explanations designed for both experienced and first-time taxpayers.

## Problem Statement
Most Nigerians including salaried workers, freelancers, and small business owners struggle to meet their tax obligations. Existing government tax portals are often desktop-oriented and difficult for first-time users to navigate, while many third-party tools focus only on tax calculations without helping users understand the broader tax process.

Tax rules are complex, key concepts like PAYE and relief allowances are poorly explained, and no single platform supports the full filing process from calculation to payment and confirmation. Users are forced across multiple, largely desktop-only systems just to complete one tax cycle.

The result is filing errors, missed deadlines, penalties, and widespread non-compliance, while tax authorities battle incomplete records and revenue shortfalls. Without a unified, accessible solution, these problems will persist for both taxpayers and government.

## Objectives
TaxEase aims to:

* Simplify tax calculation for employees, freelancers, SMEs, and first-time taxpayers.
* Improve tax transparency by providing clear, step-by-step explanations of how taxes are calculated.
* Enable users to calculate and understand their taxes through a seamless and transparent workflow.
* Reduce reliance on manual tax calculations and improve compliance with Nigerian tax regulations.
* Make tax services more accessible through a user-friendly, conversational experience and low-data usage design.
* Empower users with tax education resources, FAQs, glossaries, and timely tax updates.
* Support HR teams and payroll officers with efficient payroll tax processing and reporting tools.

## Core Features
### User Onboarding
1. Quick account setup
2. Add salary, employment, or business information
3. Personalized dashboard experience
4. Mobile-first onboarding experience

### Tax Calculator
1. Individual tax calculator for employees and freelancers
2. Input salary, pension contributions, NHF, and other deductions
3. Automatic PAYE computation
4. Net salary estimation with detailed tax breakdown
5. Batch payroll processing for multiple employees
6. Export payroll calculations to Excel or CSV

### Tax Explanation
1. Rent Relief system application and validation rules
2. Taxable income computation engine
3. Tax band breakdown and applied rates (6 progressive bands)
4. Step-by-step explanation of how tax is calculated in plain language

### Tax  Learning
1. Tax glossary for common tax terms
2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
3. Tax Learning Hub with educational resources
4. Tax filing reminders and notifications

### Reports & Exports
1. Downloadable PDF tax summaries
2. Payroll tax reports
3. Export calculation results for record-keeping and analysis

### Support 
1. In-app customer support
2. WhatsApp support channel
