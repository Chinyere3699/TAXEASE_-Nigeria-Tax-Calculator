# Taxease

## Overview
Taxease is a web and mobile-based Tax Calculator Platform designed to help Nigeria employees, freelancers, SMEs, payroll officers, HR teams, accountants and tax consultants easily and accurately calculate PAYE (Pay As You Earn) in tax, manage and understand salary deductions using Nigerian Personal Income Tax rules.

The platform simplifies tax computation through transparent calculations, payroll automation, and beginner-friendly explanations

## Problem Statement
Most Nigerians including salaried workers, freelancers, and small business owners struggle to meet their tax obligations. Tax rules are complex, key concepts like PAYE and relief allowances are poorly explained, and no single platform supports the full filing process from calculation to payment and confirmation. Users are forced across multiple, largely desktop-only systems just to complete one tax cycle.

The result is filing errors, missed deadlines, penalties, and widespread non-compliance, while tax authorities battle incomplete records and revenue shortfalls. Without a unified, accessible solution, these problems will persist for both taxpayers and government.

## Objectives
TaxEase aims to:
1. Simplify tax calculation for employees, freelancers, SMEs, and first-time taxpayers.
2. Improve tax transparency by providing clear, step-by-step explanations of how taxes are calculated.
3. Enable users to calculate, pay, and file taxes within a single seamless workflow.
4. Reduce reliance on manual tax calculations and improve compliance with Nigerian tax regulations.
5. Make tax services more accessible through a user-friendly, conversational experience and low-data usage design.
6. Empower users with tax education resources, FAQs, glossaries, and timely tax updates.
7. Support HR teams and payroll officers with efficient payroll tax processing and reporting tools.

## Core Features
### User Onboarding
1. Quick account setup
2. Add salary, employment, or business information
3. Personalized dashboard experience

### Tax Calculator
1. Individual tax calculator for employees and freelancers
2. Input salary, pension contributions, NHF, and other deductions
3. Automatic PAYE computation
4. Net salary estimation with detailed tax breakdown
5. Batch payroll processing for multiple employees
6. Export payroll calculations to Excel or CSV

### Tax Explanation
1. Consolidated Relief Allowance (CRA) calculation
2. Taxable income computation
3. Tax band breakdown and applied rates
4. Step-by-step explanation of how tax is calculated in plain language

### Tax  Learning
1. Tax glossary for common tax terms
2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
3. Tax Learning Hub with educational resources
4. Tax updates and compliance reminders

### Reports & Exports
1. Downloadable PDF tax summaries
2. Payroll tax reports
3. Export calculation results for record-keeping and analysis

### Support 
1. In-app customer support
2. WhatsApp support channel
3. Local language support
4. AI-powered Tax Assistant for quick tax-related guidance
