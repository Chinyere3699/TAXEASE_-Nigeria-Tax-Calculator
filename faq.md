# Taxease Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## Account & Profile

### How do I create a Taxease account?

To create an account, select "Register," provide the required information, verify your account, and complete your profile setup.

### Can I update my profile information later?

Yes. You can update your freelancer, employee or business owner information from your profile settings at any time.

## Tax Calculation

### What is PAYE?

PAYE (Pay As You Earn) is a tax system where income tax is deducted directly from an employee's earnings before payment is made.

### How does Taxease calculate tax?

TaxEase calculates tax based on the earning and deduction information you provide, using the applicable Nigerian PAYE tax rules and rates configured on the platform.

### Why is my net salary lower than my gross salary?

Your net salary is the amount you receive after deductions such as PAYE tax, pension contributions, NHF contributions, and other applicable deductions.

### What deductions can I include in my calculation?

Depending on your situation, you may include pension contributions, NHF contributions, and other allowable deductions supported by the platform.

**Q: What happened to the Consolidated Relief Allowance (CRA)?** A: Effective January 1, 2026, the CRA has been abolished by law. It has been replaced by the **Rent Relief system**, which allows you to deduct a portion of your actual rent paid from your taxable income base.

**Q: What is the Rent Relief system?** A: It is a tax relief mechanism for individuals who pay rent. The system automatically applies a deduction equal to the lower of 20% of your documented annual rent or ₦500,000 before your income tax is computed.


### What is taxable income?

Taxable income is the portion of your income that remains after eligible deductions and reliefs have been applied.

## Payroll Processing

### Can I calculate tax for multiple employees at once?

Yes. Payroll officers can upload employee payroll data and perform bulk PAYE calculations.

### What file format can I upload for payroll processing?

Supported file formats will be indicated on the payroll upload page. Common formats include CSV and Excel files.

### Can I export payroll results?

Yes. Payroll calculation results can be exported for reporting and record-keeping purposes
