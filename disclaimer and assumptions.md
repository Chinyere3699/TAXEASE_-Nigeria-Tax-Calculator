While reasonable efforts are made to ensure the accuracy of calculations, Taxease does not guarantee that all results will be free from errors or fully reflect individual tax circumstances.

Users are advised to consult qualified tax professionals, accountants or the appropriate tax authorities before making tax-related decisions or submissions.

TaxEase is not affiliated with or endorsed by any government tax authority unless explicitly stated.


## Assumptions

The tax calculations provided by TaxEase are based on the following assumptions:

### Income Information

* Users provide accurate and complete salary or earnings, deduction, and income information.
* All values entered are expressed in Nigerian Naira (₦).

### Tax Rules

* Calculations are based on the Nigerian Personal Income Tax framework and applicable PAYE rules configured within the platform.
* Tax rates, reliefs and deductions are applied according to the rules available at the time of calculation.

### Deductions

* Pension contributions, NHF contributions, and other deductions are entered correctly by the user.
* Only deductions supported by the platform are included in calculations.

### Calculation Results

* Results generated by the platform are estimates and may differ from final tax assessments issued by tax authorities.
* Users remain responsible for verifying the accuracy of their tax information before filing or making payments.

### Payroll Processing

* Payroll calculations are based on the employee data uploaded by payroll officers.
* Taxease assumes uploaded payroll data is accurate and complete.
