‚Äč## 1. What goods and services are exempt from VAT?
‚ÄčAnswer: To minimize the financial cost-of-living burden on citizens, the government explicitly exempts essential items from VAT. ## ‚Äč2. What is the difference between input and output VAT?
‚ÄčAnswer: Output VAT is the 10% tax that your business charges and collects from customers on your taxable sales. For example, if you collect ‚ā¶100,000 in Output VAT from sales and pay ‚ā¶40,000 in Input VAT on purchases, you will remit the remaining ‚ā¶60,000 to the government. If your Input VAT exceeds your Output VAT, you can carry the tax credit forward or file a formal refund claim with the NRS.
